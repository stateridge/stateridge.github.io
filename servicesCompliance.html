Our Contract Administration and Compliance program

Contract compliance and administration is an important part of the US Federal Government contract life-cycle. The rules of the FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulations - http://www.acquisition.gov) apply to both the Prime Contractors as well as the sub-contractor. These FAR flow-down clauses will impact your business processes including reporting and contract management.

Our services range from contract administration for the Large Business (including SBA Audits for Sub-Contracting Plans) to small business sub-contractors facing FAR flow down clauses and other compliance issues.
