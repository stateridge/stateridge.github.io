DCAA Audit

 

A Defense Contract Audit Agency Audit is an important part of winning a large US Federal Government Defense Contract. With defense spending being the highest spending category of government spending, any company that could possibly provide a service to a defense agency should be looking into the requirements. Most people associate the defense agencies with guns and rockets. This is a ill-informed view of these agencies. The defense agencies buy almost everything from companies like yours because they need a whole lot more than just guns and rockets to run a vast agency/bureaucracy and take care of troops. For certain sized contracts, a DCAA Audit becomes a requirment. We can look through your books to make sure everything is up to date with the government standards of DCAA Audits.
