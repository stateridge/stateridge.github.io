Why do we provide this service?

Writing a good proposal can be one of the most important and often most challenging parts of winning a solicitation. Articulating your solution to the US Federal Government while meeting their requirements for a compliant and competitive proposal can be the difference in winning versus losing an RFP, Request for Proposal.

Our proposal writing and management services are available a la carte by the hour and are billed in 15 minute increments. Additional fees for expedited or short turn-around times.

Project Description

We will craft your businesses proposal to be submitted in the form of a bid on a US Federal Government contract to be reviewed by a contracting officer and checked for compliance and risk.  

Project Details

  • Our proposal writing service starts with a call about the terms of the contract and performance requirments. This is neccessary to ensure the job that we are bidding for and investing resources in can be performed.
  • Our proposal writing proccess includes an executive review of the solicitation to assure compliance with guidelines and regulations mentioned in the terms of the contract. (No cost)
  • We use an eat-what-you-kill system here. We will only charge a commision of profits if we win the solicitation. This will be billed under a subcontract agreement labeled as paperwork.
  • Contract is signed.
  • When we recieve a notice stating that you have won the solication we will notify you as soon as possible.
  • Our proposal managment team will help you through managing the delivery of your product or service. (time will be billed)
  • Invoice is sent
