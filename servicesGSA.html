Background of service

We first and foremost ensure that the GSA Schedule contract vehicle/marketing tool is the right fit for your firm. We will not sell you something that you do not need or something that will be a liability for your business.

The GSA Schedule is a 5 year contract, with 3 five year renewable periods. In essence, it is a 20 year contract. The primary focus is GSA negotiating and securing the lowest rates for your goods and/or services.

The pricing analysis is based on 2 main factors:

(a) - CSP, Commercial Sales Practices and (b) - Other vendors on the GSA Schedule.

Did you know?

  • The GSA Schedule is a marketing tool only. It is NOT required to do business with the Government.
  • The GSA Schedule is one of MANY ways the Government purchases. It is used ONLY about 10-15% in federal procurements.
  • About 20,000 companies hold GSA Schedules and 63% have ZERO sales through their Schedule.
  • GSA has a $25k per year sales quota. SALES MUST BE THROUGH YOUR SCHEDULE. (First year is a grace period.) If you do not reach this quota, GSA will cancel your Schedule.
  • The main impetus of the GSA Schedule is for GSA to secure your LOWEST rates.
  • Your GSA rates are a price ceiling, or not-to-exceed rate. As you bid for contracts through your Schedule, you will typically bid lower than your GSA rate.
  • There are limitations on price increases based on your EPA, Economic Price Adjustments.
