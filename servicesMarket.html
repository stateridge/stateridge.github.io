Why do we provide this service?

The US Federal Government marketplace is unique in that so much data is available. The obstacle to success is finding the data that your business needs through the disparate government websites that are not built to be user friendly. Unfortunately, often government CO's do not standardize the solicitations so that an RFP for your solution may be placed under multiple different NAICS Codes by several different agencies all procuring your same service. We dont want you to miss opportunities! We want to help you to be strategic in your opproach through our Government Market Research and Analysis Report (GMRAR), We attempt to provide you with the most opportunities for your  product or service.

Project Description

Our Government Analysis Report (GAR) provides the following data points to guide your strategy:  

  • WHO BUYS - Federal Government Agencies purchasing your solution
    • - Name
    • - Phone #
    • - Email Address
  • WHO WON - Vendors who have won contracts for your same solution
    • (Use this for teaming, partnering, sub-contracting and competitive analysis);
  • WHEN - When the purchase occurred
      (Use this pipeline and resource planning - business development, proposal writing);
  • WHERE - Location of the purchase
      (Use this data to employ resources in areas to build relationships with buyers)
  • HOW MUCH - Dollar amount of the contract
  • HOW - Acquisition method / how your solution was procured
      - GSA Schedule - Full & Open Competition - Set-Aside - Simplified Acquisition - Sole Source - Other, etc.
      UPCOMING FORECAST - When and where available, a list of upcoming procurements for your solution is provided.
      ANALYST RECOMMENDATIONS - Based on our findings, we make custom recommendations for you to tackle the market levering the intelligence in the report.

Project Details

  • Our report comes with a 30-45 minute data gathering, fact finding call to help us better understand your business and what it can offer to the government. (No cost)
  • We provide a quotation for services. (Typically between $2-$5k)
  • Contract is signed.
  • 7-10 Business days later, we deliver the data to you
  • Our Data Analyst will spend 30-60 minutes on a phone call with you to walk through the data, how to use it and recommendations.
  • Invoice is sent
