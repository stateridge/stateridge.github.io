DUNS Number registration

DUNS registration is a crucial part of starting to do business with the federal government. A DUNS number is neccessary to have in order to participate in any one of the nearly $500 billion in contracts that the government awards each year on average. With this product, we will handle the registration proccess and you will have to wait around 30 days to recieve your DUNS number.

SAM.gov Registration

Registering for a SAM.gov account through the government account managment system, Login.gov, is a requirement with most federal contracts. Inproperly filing the SAM registration can result in account termination. This is why it is important to have a proffessional review your SAM registration so that you can avoid the negative consequences of mistakes. We will review the SAM registration of existing clients for free.
